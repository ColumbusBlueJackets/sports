Take Me Out to the Ball Game!

When it's not a hockey night in Columbus, the city shows its pride for all the local sports teams - professional and collegiate. And you can't forget about the golf!

Ohio State Football

Ohio State football is always a force to be reckoned with. When autumn comes to Columbus, the Ohio State University campus is filled with die-hard fans ready to cheer on their team to victory.

Columbus Crew

the Columbus Crew have emerged as a force in Major League Soccer, making it one of Central Ohio's most popular sports attractions. The team plays in a brand-new stadium in the Arena District, Lower.com Field.

Columbus Clippers

If you want to see the baseball stars of tomorrow, check out the Columbus Clippers, Triple-A affiliate of the Cleveland Guardians. The Clippers play at Huntington Park, named the best ballpark in America.
