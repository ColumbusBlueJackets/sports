ARTS & ENTERTAINMENT

Columbus' art scene is where you can discover the city’s cultured, fun, and playful personality.

Columbus is so much more than a destination. It’s a place where community is the heart and soul of it all. A place where parks, events, dining, shopping, nightlife and entertainment are right around every corner. Check out the best that Columbus has to offer and see for yourself!

The Dublin Market at bridge park

The 2022 Dublin Market at Bridge Park, brought to you by The OSU Wexner Medical Center, is every Saturday from 9am-12pm now through the end of September. Located on Longshore Street, you will find an assortment of goods from local farmers, makers, artisans and craftsman, along with live entertainment and more! For more information, visit our website.
